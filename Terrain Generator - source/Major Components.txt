MAJOR COMPONENTS (DLLs):
========================

X - Indicates completed function (more or less).
/ - Indicates a work in progress.
O - Indicates dropped function.

Vertex Editors (Algorithms to modify vertices):
-X Noise Generator
-X Smooth Vertices
-X Rainfall

Texture Editors (Algorithms to modify textures):
-X Combine and shift textures
-X Modify background color/texture
-X Snowfall
-/ Snowmelt

Light Editor:
-X Modify primary light color
-O Modify secondary light color
-X Modify direction of primary light
